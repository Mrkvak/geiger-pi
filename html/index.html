Geiger counter

Geiger counter is connected somewhere in Nusle district in Prague, Czechia. The connected probe is Russian SBT-10, operating at ~380V, capable of detecting alpha, beta and gamma particles. The tube's own background is around ~4CPS = 240 CPM. Times are in UTC.

Important: If you see a spike, don't panic! Most likely it means there's a loose wire somewhere, or I'm measuring something (legal! like a piece of pitchblende, uranium tableware etc...).
